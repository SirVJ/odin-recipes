Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Description

Each bite delivers a satisfying crunch followed by a rich, savory melt thatâ€™s both nostalgic and irresistible. Simple yet sublime, this grilled cheese is a timeless delight that never fails to please.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat up your pan
  2. Butter up your bread
  3. Slice your cheese
  4. Add cheese to your bread
  5. Put bread on the pan
  6. Turn over when golden brown
  7. Once both sides are gold brown, take bread off pan
  8. Done!
