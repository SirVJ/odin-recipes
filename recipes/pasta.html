Pasta

Description

Each bite is a delightful mix of umami goodness, with a hint of smokiness and a touch of savory sauce. Garnished with fresh green onions and a sprinkle of sesame seeds, this dish is a satisfying and versatile favorite that brings a touch of delicious simplicity to any meal.

Each mouthful combines the satisfying texture of the pasta with the robust depth of the sauce, creating a harmonious blend of taste and texture. Finished with a sprinkle of Parmesan and a touch of fresh basil, this dish embodies the timeless appeal of simple yet exquisite cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Jar of pasta sauce
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put water in a pot and wait till it's boiling
  2. Peel and cut your onion
  3. Peel and cut your garlic
  4. Put pasta in boiling till cooked
  5. Heat up and oil pan
  6. Add onions and garlic
  7. Add jar of pasta sauce
  8. Add seasonings to taste
  9. Add cooked pasta
  10. Done!
