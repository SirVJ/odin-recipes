Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Savor the vibrant flavors of our Savory Fried Rice, a stir-fried symphony of fluffy jasmine rice, tender vegetables, and succulent bits of protein.

Each bite is a delightful mix of umami goodness, with a hint of smokiness and a touch of savory sauce. Garnished with fresh green onions and a sprinkle of sesame seeds, this dish is a satisfying and versatile favorite that brings a touch of delicious simplicity to any meal.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • 1x Onion
  • 2x Garlic Cloves
  • 2x Carrots
  • Frozen Peas
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook some rice
  2. Peel and cut your onion
  3. Peel and cut your garlic
  4. Cut carrots into small squares
  5. Heat up a frying pan or wok
  6. Add onions and garlic and fry until golden brown
  7. Add frozen peas
  8. Add carrots
  9. Add rice
  10. Add seasonings to taste
  11. Done!
